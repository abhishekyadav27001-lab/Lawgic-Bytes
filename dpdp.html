Section 2: Definitions & Roles

Data Principal

"The Owner"

Section 2(j): The individual to whom the personal data relates. Includes parents/guardians for children or persons with disabilities.

Data Fiduciary

"The Keeper"

Section 2(i): The entity (e.g., Bank, App, State) that determines the purpose and means of processing personal data.

Data Processor

"The Worker"

Section 2(k): Any person who processes personal data on behalf of a Data Fiduciary.

Consent Manager

"The Agent"

Section 2(g) & Rule 4: A registered entity acting as a single point of contact to give, manage, review, and withdraw consent.
